Matt Winzer: Budding Web Developer

I'm a fun loving, laid-back guy who wants to change the world, one line of code at a time!

I believe that computers and technology are, quite simply, incredible. They are one of mankind's greatest creations, largely because of their ability to bring people together. With the power of the modern web, the impact that computers have on our lives is only increasing. After a lifetime of being an enthusiast and a few years of being an amateur, I've decided that I want to learn how to truly become a master of these magical instruments!


I've recently recognized a deep desire to create, and to build. I see web development as a quintessential act of creation, and I couldn't be more excited to learn from some of the best at Galvanize!
